Week 1 · April 22

Retreat Reflections

A weekend away from campus brought the class together and celebrate the first week. Twelve people. New dynamics. Honest conversations.

Class Reflection

"The retreat gave us a chance to step away from the pressure of the program and actually get to know each other. We played games, shared stories, and had the kinds of honest conversations that we didn't have a chance to have before. It also gave us the space to align on our approach going into the Fundraiser pitch presentation, so we left not just closer, but more prepared. In parallel we also began our Product Strategy Presentation which has its progress on the Our Work Page."
