Why We Serve

Precision, Unity, and Purpose — Extended Beyond the Program

Our class identity is built on the belief that great business leaders don't stop at the door of the boardroom. The same drive that pushes us through presentations and fundraisers also pushes us to show up for others. Community service is not a checkbox — it is a reflection of who Xidiac actually is.


Just as the zodiac cycle does not exist in isolation — each animal part of something larger — our class exists within a community. This page documents our contribution to it.
