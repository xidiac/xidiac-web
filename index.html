Who We Are

Developing Precision, Unity,
Forward Momentum, and Discipline
Together as a Team.

Xidiac is a collective of twelve individuals unified through ambition, structure, and purpose. Inspired by the Chinese Zodiac — twelve distinct paths, one cohesive cycle — the brand emphasizes individuality within a system. Each member contributes something unique to the whole. Just as the zodiac cycle is rooted in tradition yet moves forward with time, we aim to respect the foundations that guide us while continuously evolving as future business leaders.
