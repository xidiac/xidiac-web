Who We Are

A Collective Built on Ambition, Structure, and Purpose

Xidiac represents a collective of twelve individuals unified through ambition, structure, and purpose. We are the Xi Pledge Class of Spring 2026 — twelve distinct people, each bringing a different background, a different perspective, and a different path.

Our journey didn't begin with a shared hobby or a common interest. In fact, the more we searched for sameness, the more we felt stuck. The revelation came when we stopped looking for what made us alike and leaned into what made us different.

"We discovered that while our backgrounds vary, our unified drive as future business leaders is what binds us."

This insight led us to the Chinese Zodiac as our guiding framework: twelve distinct animals, each unique, yet all part of one cohesive cycle. Just as no two animals are alike, no two members of Xidiac are alike — and that is precisely our strength.

What We Stand For

Twelve Paths.
One Direction.

I
Individuality Within Unity

Twelve individuals, twelve distinct paths — but one unified direction. We do not ask our members to be the same. We ask them to be fully themselves, and trust that together, those differences create something greater than any single part.

II
Strength Through Difference

Our differences are not gaps to be bridged — they are our greatest resource. Finance minds sit alongside creative strategists. Entrepreneurs collaborate with consultants. That diversity of thought is exactly what future business leaders need.

III
Rooted in Tradition

Inspired by the Chinese Zodiac — twelve animals, each carrying distinct energy and symbolism — we draw on a framework thousands of years old to remind us that cycles, structure, and identity are timeless. Tradition gives us roots.

IV
Forward Momentum

Precision, unity, forward momentum, and discipline — together as a team. The zodiac moves forward. So do we. Respecting the foundations that guide us while continuously evolving is not a contradiction; it is the only way to grow.

Our Structure

Twelve Members.
Twelve Animals.
One Logo.

Each of the twelve members of Xidiac is assigned a zodiac animal — not arbitrarily, but as a symbol of their role within the class. The same twelve nodes that appear in our logo each correspond to a person. The symbol is intentional: every time you see the XI monogram, you see all of us.


The circular logo system — with its golden ring and 12 outer nodes — mirrors the zodiac cycle itself. A continuous loop with no beginning and no end, where every position holds equal weight.
